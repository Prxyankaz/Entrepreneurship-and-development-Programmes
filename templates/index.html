ENTREPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS

HOME PROGRAMS REGISTRATION UPDATE PROFILE

Entrepreneurship promotion and development programmes are designed to nurture the talent of youth by enlightening them on various aspects of industrial/business activity required for setting up MSEs.

These programmes are conducted for youth and other people interested in establishing their own industrial/self-employment venture. Such activities are also organized in ITIs, Polytechnics, and other technical institutions/business schools, where skill/talent is available to motivate them towards self-employment.

Our goal is to help you transform your innovative ideas into successful businesses through structured training, mentorship, and resources.
